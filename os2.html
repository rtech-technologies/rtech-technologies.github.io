OSx2 Limemade

The Sovereign Choice Manifesto

I The Vision

OSx2 is a 64-bit operating system built from the ground up on one simple truth: You own the machine. Unlike modern operating systems that are bloated with millions of lines of "spyware" and unnecessary complexity, OSx2 is a "Sovereign" environment. It is lightweight, "Boringly Stable," and gives you total control over every transistor in your computer.

II The Three Sovereign Pillars

1. Smart Power (The 1-per-6 Rule)

OSx2 respects your hardware by using a 1-core-per-6-tasks ratio. As you open more apps, OSx2 "ignites" more power. When you close them, the hardware goes back to sleep, saving energy and maintaining optimal temperatures.

2. The Clean-Sweep Safety (SAFE_load_Task)

Every app runs in a isolated "Safe Chamber". If an app breaks a rule or crashes, OSx2 terminates it instantly. Upon closure, OSx2 "scrubs" the RAM, sanitizing every bit of memory and returning it to the system.

3. RTECH Standard (The Language of People)

Utilizing RSL (RTECH Standard Language), OSx2 offers a simplistic, one-line-at-a-time syntax. Configuration is handled via direct orders rather than complex menus, providing accessibility without sacrificing power.
